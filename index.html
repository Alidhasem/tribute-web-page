Steven Wilson

The prodigal son of progressive rock

Who is Steven Wilson?

Steven Wilson is a English musician, songwriter and producer. He has a succesful solo career, with albums like "The Raven that refused to sing" and "Hand Cannot Erase". And not only that, he also has participated in recognized bands like Porcupine Tree, Blackfield, Storm-Corrosion and No-Man.

Awards and honors

  • Steven Wilson has been nominated for Grammy Awards: once as a solo artist, twice with Porcupine Tree, and once with Storm Corrosion.
  • In 2016, the Guitar World magazine named him one of the 15 best progressive rock guitarists in the history of this genere.
  • Steven Wilson was awarded the UK band/artist of the year by the Prog magazine in 2018

Colaborations

Steven Wilson has worked with artists such as Elton John, XTC, Opeth, Pendulum, Yes, Fish, Marillion, Black Sabbath, and Anathema. As a producer he has remixed several classic pop and rock records, such as Tears for Fears' Songs From The Big Chair, Jethro Tull's Aqualung and In the Court of the Crimson King by King Crimson.
